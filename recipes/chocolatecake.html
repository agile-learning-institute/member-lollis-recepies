Description

This a recipe to make a delicious chocolate cake

Ingrediants

  • 1 1/2 cups of flour
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 cup of cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1/2 cup of oil
  • 1 tsp of baking powder
  • 1 tsp of vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Steps

  1. preheat oven to 450 degress
  2. mix all dry ingrediants in large bowl
  3. add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract then mix until smooth.
  4. Pour batter into a greased bake pan and bake for 30 mins
  5. let cake cool before serving
