Description

This a recipe is gonna tell you how to make a delicious key lime pie

Ingrediants

  • 5 large egg yolks beatean
  • 1 (14 oz) condensed milk
  • 1/2 cup of key lime juice
    • 1 (9inch) prepared graham cracker crust

Steps

    grab all ingrediants
  1. preheat oven to 375 degress F
  2. combine sweetened condensed milk, key lime juice, and egg yolks in large bowl; mix well.
  3. pour mixture into unbaked graham cracker crust.
  4. bake in preheated oven until filling is set, about 15 mins.
    5. allow to cool completely before slicing
